Off - Grid

Off-grid solar systems provide independent power generation, ideal for remote locations or areas with unreliable grid access. These systems utilize batteries to store excess energy for use during periods of low sunlight or at night, offering sustainable and reliable electricity off the grid.

Hybrid

Hybrid solar systems combine the benefits of both on-grid and off-grid systems, offering flexibility and reliability. These systems incorporate battery storage to store excess solar energy for use during peak demand or in the event of a grid outage, providing backup power and reducing reliance on the main electricity grid.

Grid - Tied

On-grid solar systems are connected to the main electricity grid, allowing users to both consume solar-generated electricity and sell excess power back to the grid. These systems offer cost savings through net metering and contribute to a more sustainable energy grid by harnessing renewable solar energy.
